Privacy Policy for Prank Ya Friend

This Privacy Policy describes how Prank Ya Friend ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our browser extension.

Information Collection

Our extension uses the following permissions:

  • activeTab: We only access the current tab when you interact with our extension. This is used to modify link behaviors for the prank functionality.
  • storage: We store your extension preferences locally on your device (such as randomness degree settings). This data never leaves your device.
  • scripting: This permission allows our extension to modify webpage behavior for the prank functionality.

Data Usage

We do not:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Track your browsing history
  • Store any data on external servers
  • Share any information with third parties

Local Storage

The only data we store are your extension preferences, which are kept locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This includes:

  • Randomness degree settings
  • Extension enable/disable status

Website Access

Our extension can run on all websites (<all_urls>) to provide the prank functionality. However, it:

  • Only activates when the extension is enabled
  • Only affects link clicking behavior
  • Does not collect or transmit any website data

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the extension accordingly.
